Owner's Biography

Jacob Staley is the proud founder of UH Eats! With over 10 years of experience as a professional chef and watching Youtube tutorials, Staley is ready to revitalize campus cuisine. UH Eats is the place to find delicous food on a college student's budget!

Staley is also an active member of the community, often working in charity events and food drives to feed those less fortunate. He has been known to contribute much food to donations, and extra production is always donated to local shelters.
